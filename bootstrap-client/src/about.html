Hi, I'm Genevieve Miller

I am a Licensed Professional Counselor and Founder of Spirit Wisdom Counseling. I am a soul counselor who is spiritually sensitive and I have spent a long time trying to figure out where I fit in the world. I started this practice to give strength and light to people who have always felt different and have struggled to figure out why. I am passionate about helping people begin to listen to their inner wisdom and use their gifts as a strength. Throughout my experience, I have found that people mostly want to be loved, they want to be heard, and they want to be seen.

My areas of concentration include spirituality, substance use, and women's issues. I use evidenced-based practices such as emotionally-focused therapy, CBT, and DBT to inform my work. I also integrate intuition and a mind, body, spirit approach.

I am a human who has been "stuck" and who has fumbled through many of life's challenges. I have taken leaps of faith and been terrified, I have failed, and have experienced life completely turned upside down. I am human and so are you. This is where the work happens, and this is the reason why I do what I do. I'm glad you're here.
