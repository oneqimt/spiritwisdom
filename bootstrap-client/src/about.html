A few words about me

We help people heal with respect

I respect the space we share in the therapy room and I believe therapy is a collaborative process.

I have several years of experience working in community mental health settings and in the field of substance use/abuse. I am a strong advocate for my clients and I believe it is important to honor your unique experiences.

I admire my clients for the very diverse and beautiful human beings they are. I want to make sure I acknowledge your identities and create a safe space for you to explore more of who you are in therapy.
